# Privacy Policy for Solaire

**Effective Date:** January 8, 2026  
**Last Updated:** January 8, 2026

## Introduction

Solaire ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application handles information when you use our service.

## App Description

Solaire is a mobile application that helps users find the nearest sun location based on geographic coordinates selected on an interactive map.

## Information We Do NOT Collect

Solaire is designed with privacy in mind. We do NOT:

- Collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII)
- Track user behavior or analytics
- Request access to your device's location services
- Create user accounts or profiles
- Use cookies or similar tracking technologies
- Share any data with third parties
- Store any user data on our servers beyond what is necessary to process your current session request

## Information We Process Temporarily

When you use Solaire, the following information is processed temporarily to provide the service:

- **Geographic Coordinates**: When you tap on the map, the latitude and longitude coordinates are sent to our server to calculate and return sun location results. These coordinates are processed in real-time and are not stored permanently.

## How We Use Information

The geographic coordinates you select are used solely to:

- Calculate and return the nearest sun locations within the specified search radius
- Process your search request in real-time

Once your search request is complete, the data is not retained or stored.

## Third-Party Services

Solaire uses the following third-party services that may have their own privacy policies:

- **Flutter Map**: An open-source mapping library. Map tiles are fetched from OpenStreetMap or similar tile providers.
- **OpenStreetMap**: Map data is provided by OpenStreetMap contributors. Please refer to [OpenStreetMap's Privacy Policy](https://wiki.osmfoundation.org/wiki/Privacy_Policy) for more information.
- **Open-Meteo**: Weather data is provided by OpenMeteo.  Please refer to [Open-Meteo's Terms and Privacy Policy](https://open-meteo.com/en/terms) for more information.
## Internet Connectivity

Solaire requires an internet connection to:

- Display map tiles
- Communicate with our server to fetch sun location data

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect the limited data we process during your use of the app. All communication with our servers is conducted over secure connections.

## Children's Privacy

Solaire does not knowingly collect or solicit any information from anyone under the age of 13. The app is not directed to children under 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

## International Users

Solaire can be used worldwide. By using the app, you consent to the processing of your search coordinates as described in this Privacy Policy.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect or store personal information, there is no user data to access, modify, or delete. Each search is processed independently without any data retention.
